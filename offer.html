Frequently Asked

How are remote sessions conducted?

Sessions are conducted via Zoom, Skype, or Google Meet. You need only a computer with internet connection, camera, and microphone. All materials are provided digitally.

May I reschedule a session?

Certainly. Provide 24 hours notice and we shall arrange an alternative time without additional charge.

Timeline from beginner to B2 proficiency?

With regular sessions (twice weekly) and dedicated self-study, achieving B2 level typically requires 12-18 months. Individual progress varies naturally.

What payment methods are accepted?

Bank transfer or BLIK. VAT invoices provided upon request.
